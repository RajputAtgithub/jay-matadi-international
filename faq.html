General Questions

Jay Matadi International is a leading provider of premium fresh fruits, container logistics services, and corrugated packaging solutions. Founded in 2005, we've grown to become a trusted partner for businesses worldwide in the fresh food supply chain industry.

Our headquarters and main operations facility is located at 123 Business Avenue, Industrial Area, City, Country. We also have satellite offices and partner facilities in key agricultural and logistics hubs around the world.

We primarily serve the food and agriculture industry, but our services are also utilized by e-commerce businesses, retail chains, pharmaceutical companies, and other industries that require fresh produce, temperature-controlled logistics, or specialized packaging solutions.

We maintain several important certifications including ISO 9001 for quality management, HACCP for food safety, GlobalGAP for good agricultural practices, and various international phytosanitary certifications for our fresh fruit exports.

Fresh Fruits

We offer a wide variety of tropical, subtropical, and exotic fruits including mangoes, pineapples, bananas, papayas, oranges, avocados, lemons, dragon fruit, rambutan, and mangosteen. Our product range varies by season.

We implement strict quality control measures at every stage from farm selection to final delivery. This includes optimal harvesting times, careful handling, multiple inspection points, proper packaging, and temperature-controlled transportation.

Yes, we offer certified organic options for several of our fruit varieties. These are grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers and meet international organic certification standards.

Minimum order quantities vary by product and destination. For full container loads (FCL), we typically require a minimum of one 20ft or 40ft reefer container. For less than container loads (LCL), minimums depend on the specific fruit and shipping method.

Container Logistics

We offer various container types including 20ft and 40ft reefer containers (temperature-controlled), standard dry containers, high cube containers, and specialized containers for specific cargo needs.

Our reefer containers are equipped with advanced monitoring systems that track and record temperature, humidity, and other environmental conditions throughout the journey. This data is accessible to you in real-time through our tracking portal.

We service major shipping routes worldwide, with particular expertise in routes between tropical fruit-producing regions and markets in North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. We can arrange shipping to virtually any port worldwide.

Yes, we provide comprehensive customs clearance services including documentation preparation, duty calculation, and coordination with customs authorities in both origin and destination countries.

Packaging

We produce a wide range of corrugated boxes including regular slotted containers, die-cut boxes, full overlap boxes, multi-depth boxes, printed boxes, and eco-friendly boxes. We offer single-wall, double-wall, and triple-wall constructions in various flute profiles.

Absolutely. Our design team can create custom packaging solutions tailored to your specific product dimensions, protection requirements, and branding needs. We offer structural design, printing, and specialty finishes.

Standard lead time for custom orders is 2-3 weeks from design approval, depending on complexity and quantity. Rush services may be available for an additional fee. Stock items typically ship within 3-5 business days.

Yes, we offer several sustainable packaging options including boxes made from recycled materials, biodegradable coatings, and reduced-material designs that maintain strength while minimizing environmental impact.

Orders & Payments

You can place an order by contacting our sales team via phone, email, or through our website's contact form. For new customers, we'll begin with a consultation to understand your needs before providing a formal quote.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, letters of credit, and for established customers, we may offer credit terms. Payment methods and terms are typically confirmed at the time of order.

Yes, we offer volume discounts for large orders across all our services. The specific discount structure depends on the product/service, order quantity, and frequency. Contact our sales team for details.

For fresh fruits, we don't accept returns due to perishability but will address any quality issues promptly. For packaging products, returns may be accepted for manufacturing defects. Logistics services are non-refundable once rendered. Specific terms are outlined in our sales contracts.
