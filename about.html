About Jay Matadi International

Our story, mission, and values that drive our success

Our History

Founded in 2005, Jay Matadi International began as a small fresh fruit export business with a vision to deliver premium quality products to international markets. Over the years, we've expanded our services to include container logistics and packaging solutions, becoming a comprehensive service provider in the food supply chain industry.

2005

Company founded with focus on fresh fruit exports

2010

Expanded to include container logistics services

2015

Established corrugated box manufacturing facility

2020

Recognized as industry leader with multiple awards

Our Mission

To provide exceptional quality fresh foods and reliable services through sustainable practices, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in integrated fresh food supply chain solutions, recognized for excellence in quality, service, and innovation.

Core Values

Sustainability
