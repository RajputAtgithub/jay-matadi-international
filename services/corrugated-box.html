Material Options

Single Wall

  • One fluted medium between two liners
  • Common flute profiles: A, B, C, E, F
  • Good balance of strength and cost
  • Ideal for: Light to medium weight products

Double Wall

  • Two fluted mediums with three liners
  • Common combinations: BC, EB
  • Increased stacking strength
  • Ideal for: Heavy or fragile items

Triple Wall

  • Three fluted mediums with four liners
  • Extreme durability
  • Can replace wooden crates
  • Ideal for: Industrial shipments

Quality Assurance

Every box we produce undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards and your specific requirements. Our quality control process includes:

  • Raw material inspection
  • In-process quality checks
  • Final product inspection
  • Bursting strength tests

  • Print quality verification
  • Dimensional accuracy checks

We maintain detailed quality records for every production run to ensure consistency and traceability.
