Exceptional Quality Fresh Fruits

At Jay Matadi International, we specialize in sourcing and exporting premium quality fresh fruits from carefully selected farms. Our rigorous quality control processes ensure that only the best produce reaches your customers, maintaining freshness, flavor, and nutritional value.

Our Fruit Categories

Mangoes

Sweet, juicy mangoes available in multiple varieties including Alphonso, Kent, and Keitt.

Available: May - September

Pineapples

Sweet and tangy pineapples with perfect balance of acidity and sweetness.

Available: Year-round

Bananas

Premium Cavendish bananas with consistent quality and perfect ripeness.

Available: Year-round

Papayas

Sweet, buttery papayas with vibrant orange flesh and high nutritional value.

Available: March - November

Oranges

Juicy, sweet oranges with high vitamin C content and excellent shelf life.

Available: November - April

Avocados

Available: Year-round

Lemons

Available: Year-round

Dragon Fruit
